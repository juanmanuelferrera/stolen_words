About the Audiobook

In 1972, A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupāda gave the world his Bhagavad-gītā As It Is. After his passing in 1977, something extraordinary happened to his work—it was thoroughly rewritten.

This audiobook documents the literary crime of the century: how 77% of verses were altered, how millions of readers never knew, and what it means when sacred texts undergo wholesale transformation after an author's death.
